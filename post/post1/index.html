2/28 - Received email reply from Jonas Prellburg regarding the C-NMC Dataset.

“I won’t be available for a Zoom call but I wanted to give you some quick pointers anyway:

AFAIK the dataset really is the biggest publicly available ALL dataset so it’s a good choice to work with. It’s size is also what makes it amenable to deep learning approaches. Many other datasets of just 100s of images are too small to use properly and the approaches then heavily rely on pre-training on general image datasets.

The dataset unfortunately also has some flaws, e.g. it is already processed by segmentation algorithms and the raw data is unavailable publically.

I’m sure you have already found it but there is a proceedings article that contains other challenge entry descriptions here that you might look at for more details and inspiration: https://nam04.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.springer.com%2Fgp%2Fbook%2F9789811507977&amp;data=04%7C01%7C830810%40locker.lcps.org%7C6bda366ed68840e2cf6108d8dc08627a%7C86750b0cee12463db28880f7b2b34689%7C0%7C0%7C637501275912562813%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C1000&amp;sdata=5xvG6RZvJ9qQxPClkWCNbf1E1xwuizDHey9eYN3sr%2F4%3D&amp;reserved=0
